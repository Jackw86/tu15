We will never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

5. Data retention

  • Active accounts: data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: all personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Team data: posts, RSVPs, messages linked to your account are anonymised or removed on account deletion
  • Payment records: Stripe retains billing records for 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Backups: may persist for up to 90 days in encrypted offline backups before being overwritten

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data (edit in-app)
  • Erasure — delete your account and all data (available in-app under Profile → Delete account)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — for push notifications (via device settings)

To exercise any right, email privacy@yourapp.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your local supervisory authority.

7. Children's privacy (COPPA & UK)

TeamUp is designed for team management by adults (coaches, parents, club administrators). If your team includes players under 13 (US) or under 13 (UK), the team admin (adult) is responsible for obtaining appropriate parental consent before adding minors to the team.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data without parental consent, contact us at privacy@yourapp.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

  • All data transmitted over TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)
  • Firestore Security Rules enforce per-user and per-team access controls
  • Firebase Storage rules restrict file access to team members only
  • Push tokens stored encrypted at rest in Firestore
  • Stripe handles all payment card data — we never store card details
  • We conduct periodic security reviews of our Firebase rules

9. International transfers

Your data is stored in Google Cloud europe-west2 (London). For EU users, standard contractual clauses (SCCs) apply for any transfers outside the EEA via our sub-processors. Stripe processes data in the US under SCCs and is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

10. Cookies & tracking

The TeamUp mobile app does not use cookies. Our website may use strictly necessary cookies only (no advertising or analytics cookies without consent). We do not use cross-app tracking technologies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via push notification or email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of TeamUp after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
